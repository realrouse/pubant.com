How to Visit Websites on Autonomi

  1. Start AntTP:
    Follow the instructions on how to install AntTP.
    Make sure you have the anttp binary running on your device. This lets your browser connect to the network.
  2. Visit a Site:
    Enter a website address into your browser, such as:
    localhost:8080/0cebc6aae92c845cecddb41fb33aae3251c7aaf97d0cf47c33630cb081e315e1/site-temp/index.html
    or other addresses shared by the community.
  3. Bookmark Your Favorites:
    Decentralized websites may have long addresses. Bookmark the ones you like for easy access later!
