What is the Autonomi Network?

A decentralized, censorship-proof network that gives control back to the users — not corporations or governments.

Visit Autonomi.com for info, or Discord to chat.

What Makes Autonomi Different?

The Autonomi Network is a fully decentralized, peer-to-peer internet where websites, apps, and data are stored across a global network of users instead of centralized servers. There’s no single point of failure, and no central authority that can control or remove content.

Key Features

  • Truly Decentralized: No servers. No gatekeepers. Content is distributed across thousands of nodes worldwide.
  • Permanent Storage: Once uploaded, your site lives forever. It can't be taken down by anyone.
  • Anonymous and Secure: Your identity is protected, and your data is safe from surveillance or censorship.
  • Community Powered: Owned and run by its users, not by corporations or centralized companies.
  • Open Source: Autonomi is built with transparency and open collaboration at its heart.

Why It Matters

In today’s web, large companies and governments control most of the data. They can track you, block access, and even erase your content. Autonomi changes that forever — giving you true freedom to create, share, and access information without restrictions.

How Autonomi Works

By using decentralized technologies, Autonomi splits and distributes files across the network. No one owns your data but you. When you publish a site, it becomes part of the network’s shared storage — accessible by anyone, anytime.

You don’t need a traditional web hosting company. You don't even need permission. Once your content is on the network, it's available globally, permanently.
