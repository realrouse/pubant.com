What is ANT?

The Ant Token (ANT) powers the Autonomi Network — securing the system, enabling uploads, and rewarding the community.

What is ANT?

ANT (Ant Token) is the digital token that fuels the Autonomi Network. It’s used to pay for uploading content, incentivizing network participants, and ensuring that the network remains robust, decentralized, and spam-free.

ANT Specifications:

  • Blockchain Network: Arbitrum One (ETH L2)
  • ERC20 Standard
  • Token Contract Address:
    0xa78d8321B20c4Ef90eCd72f2588AA985A4BDb684 EtherScan
  • Price Stats at DexTools

What ANT is Used For

  • Publishing Content: You use a small amount of ANT when you upload websites, files, or apps to the network.
  • Incentivizing Nodes: Network operators (people who run nodes) are rewarded with ANT for helping to store and serve content.
  • Preventing Spam: By requiring a minimal cost to upload, ANT protects the network from spam and abuse.
  • Economic Security: ANT encourages honest participation, making attacks expensive and unprofitable.

How to Get ANT

ANT can be earned by contributing storage and bandwidth to the network as a Node runner, or obtained through decentralized or centralized cryptocurrency exchanges. The network is designed to be open and fair for all participants.

Why ANT Matters

Without ANT, the network couldn’t function. It balances incentives and ensures that Autonomi stays self-sustaining, decentralized, and resistant to abuse — empowering users without relying on any central authority.
